Detecting Pneumonia From X-Ray Images:

Pneumonia is an infection that inflames the air
sacs in one or both lungs. The air sacs may fill
with fluid or pus, causing cough with phlegm
or pus, fever, chills, and difficulty breathing.
A variety of organisms, including bacteria,
viruses and fungi, can cause pneumonia.
